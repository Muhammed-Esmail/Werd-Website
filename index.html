FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

1 Does Werd work offline?

Yes! Werd is designed to work 100% offline. All Quran text and features are available without an internet connection.

2 Can I use Werd with a physical Mushaf?

Absolutely! Werd supports manual override for physical Mushaf readers. The app displays your Werd's page range (from page X to page Y) so you know exactly where to start and stop.

3 What happens if I miss a day?

Don't worry! Werd automatically recalculates your reading plan if you fall behind. Your journey continues without pressure.

4 Is Werd free to use?

Yes, Werd is completely free. Our mission is to help Muslims maintain their Quran reading habit without any barriers.

5 Can I share my daily Werd with others?

Yes! You can download your daily Werd as a beautifully formatted PDF and share it with friends, family, or on social media.
